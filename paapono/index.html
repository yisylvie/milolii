Welcome to the Future of Miloliʻi

The Paʻa Pono Miloliʻi Enrichment, Cultural, & Community Center is currently under construction — a long-awaited space designed to uplift our keiki, strengthen our ʻohana, and preserve the cultural legacy of Miloliʻi for generations to come.

This center is more than a building. It is a commitment to our community’s resilience, identity, and future.

Our Purpose

The center is envisioned to serve as a safe, vibrant, and culturally grounded hub where community members of all ages can learn, gather, and thrive.

Our Core Goals

  • Preserve and perpetuate Miloliʻi’s culture through education, storytelling, and hands-on practice.
  • Provide enrichment programs for keiki, kūpuna, and ʻohana.
  • Support community well-being through resources, workshops, and events.
  • Strengthen food sovereignty and sustainability.
  • Create a central gathering place for meetings, celebrations, and cultural protocol.
