Hoʻomau

In 1980, Pa‘a Pono Miloli‘i was formed to improve the quality of life for the residents of Miloliʻi—a mission we are committed to to this day.

Paʻa Pono Miloliʻi is dedicated to keeping fishing practices and cultural traditions alive for future generations. We humbly ask for your support to help us continue that mission.
